## GloBIAS Bioimage Analysts Workshop

In November 2024, I attended the [**GloBIAS Bioimage Analysts Workshop**](https://www.globias.org/activities/past-activties/annual-workshop-gothenburg-2024) in Gothenburg, Sweden.

The workshop brought together bioimage analysts and community members working on imaging workflows, training, infrastructure, and shared challenges across the field.

### Highlights
- Participated in the annual GloBIAS community workshop.
- Joined discussions on current needs and directions in bioimage analysis.
- Connected with the wider international bioimage analysis community.
