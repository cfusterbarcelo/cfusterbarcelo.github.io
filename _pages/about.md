## Who I am
I’m a Postdoctoral Researcher at the [BioVision Center, University of Zurich](https://www.biovisioncenter.uzh.ch/en.html), working with [Dr. Virginie Uhlmann](https://www.biovisioncenter.uzh.ch/en/people/team/virginie.html) on machine learning for **bioimage analysis**. I earned my PhD (*cum laude*) at [Universidad Carlos III de Madrid (UC3M)](https://www.uc3m.es/) and later held a postdoc in its [Bioengineering Department](https://www.uc3m.es/bioengineering-department/home). I actively contribute to [AI4Life](https://ai4life.eurobioimaging.eu/), support the [BioImage Model Zoo](https://bioimage.io/), and help build user-friendly tools like [deepImageJ](https://deepimagej.github.io/) and [SAMJ](https://github.com/segment-anything-models-java/SAMJ-IJ) to make cutting-edge methods accessible to the community.

If you’d like to reach out—whether to collaborate or to get help with any of these tools—don’t hesitate! I’m happy to assist directly or point you to the right person.



## Research
My main interests are:
- Bioimage analysis
- Deep Learning
- Bioengineering
