###  English

In March 2025, I gave a talk titled **"Misinformation and Telecom Antennas: Fighting Fear with Science"** as part of the *Community Talks* program by [T3chFest](https://t3chfest.uc3m.es/2025/).

The talk was born from a very personal experience: speaking in **Felanitx**, my hometown, to address local concerns about 5G antennas. Despite some nerves, I shared what science says about how antennas work, what non-ionizing radiation really is, and how safety standards are defined and enforced.

Topics included:

- What a telecom antenna actually is  
- How electromagnetic waves function  
- Why there's no scientific evidence that 5G is dangerous  
- How to respond to misinformation with empathy and facts  

 **Slides and materials**: [Zenodo (10.5281/zenodo.15016466)](https://zenodo.org/records/15016466)

This talk aimed to empower the public with accessible scientific knowledge, debunk myths, and encourage informed decisions free from irrational fear.
