In May 2025, I participated in the [EPFL Open Microscopy & AI Workshop](https://www.epfl-open-microscopy.com), where I gave a presentation and led a hands-on workshop on **Deep Learning for Bioimage Analysis**, aligned with the [AI4Life](https://ai4life.eurobioimaging.eu/) initiative.

The session included:

- Presentation on AI workflows in microscopy following the AI4Life approach
- Hands-on guide using **deepImageJ** for image segmentation
- Interactive image annotation with **SAMJ**, powered by the Segment Anything Model
- Practical tools and strategies for deploying DL models in ImageJ/Fiji
