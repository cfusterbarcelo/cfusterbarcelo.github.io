In November 2023, I presented the poster **"Estimación de la proporción de hidrops endolinfático en pacientes con la enfermedad de Mènière"** at [CASEIB 2023](https://caseib.es/2023/) in Cartagena, Spain.

The work was based on the Bachelor Thesis project by Gloria Delicado-Correa and focused on deep learning methods to estimate endolymphatic hydrops ratios in patients with Ménière’s disease from MRI data.
