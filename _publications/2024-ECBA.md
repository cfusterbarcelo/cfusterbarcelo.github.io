## Harnessing Deep Learning in Bioimaging: The DeepImageJ Journey

**Presented at**: [Effectively Communicating Bioimage Analysis Workshop](https://www.biologists.com/workshops/february-2024/), East Sussex, UK  
**Organized by**: [The Company of Biologists](https://www.biologists.com) and [Focal Plane](https://focalplane.biologists.com)

---

###  Abstract Summary

This talk presents the trajectory of *DeepImageJ*, a user-friendly plugin bridging the gap between advanced deep learning and biological imaging workflows in Fiji/ImageJ. The abstract outlines milestones of integration, compatibility enhancements, and real-world adoption in the life sciences.

The session focused on effectively **communicating technical tools to biology-focused audiences**, enhancing user understanding and engagement.
