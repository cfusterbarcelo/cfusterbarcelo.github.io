**Type**: Resource

The BioImage Model Zoo is a community resource for publishing, discovering, testing, and reusing pre-trained models across microscopy and bioimage analysis workflows.

I contributed to the BioImage Model Zoo during the AI4Life project, where I was in charge of user support and documentation. I also contributed to the development of the BioImage Model Zoo by providing feedback on the user interface and testing new features.

Nowadays, I still contribute helping users, testing new features, and providing feedback to the developers.
