## deepImageJ

**Type**: Software

---

deepImageJ is a Fiji/ImageJ plugin designed to make state-of-the-art deep learning models usable in everyday microscopy and image analysis workflows without requiring users to write code.

My contributions have supported the broader deepImageJ effort around accessible AI deployment, dissemination, and integration into community-facing microscopy workflows.
