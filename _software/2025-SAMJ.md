## SAMJ

**Type**: Software  
**My role**: Co-developer  
**Tool**: [SAMJ repository](https://github.com/segment-anything-models-java/SAMJ-IJ)  
**Documentation**: [SAMJ wiki](https://github.com/segment-anything-models-java/SAMJ-IJ/wiki)  
**Associated paper**: [SAMJ: Fast Image Annotation on ImageJ/Fiji via Segment Anything Model]({{ page.paper_path | relative_url }})  
**Associated talk**: [Transformers, Vision Transformers and SAMJ]({{ page.talk_path | relative_url }})  

---

SAMJ integrates Segment Anything models into the Fiji/ImageJ ecosystem, enabling fast and interactive annotation workflows for microscopy and image analysis.

My work on SAMJ has focused on helping make modern segmentation and annotation methods accessible in practical bioimage analysis environments used by the community.
